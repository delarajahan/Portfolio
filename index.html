Well good morning everyone and welcome to my website!

I am very excited and nervous at the same time to share the details of my very simple life. I'm Delara Jahan. And I'm a housewife.

I am a married stay at home Mom of a son, Aiden 6, and a daughter, Madelynn 3. We try to live a very fit and active life. I pretty much spend 80% of my time taking care of and spending time with my family, about another 10% working out (in the last three years I have managed to lose 20lbs and keep it off), and the last 10% goes to well I hate admit television. I am a real housewife loving, ID channel obsessed and Scandal loving Momma. I am also short, hence the word short not the title. 5'1" and still waiting to hit my growth spurt.

About my little family 1. My husband Mr.C is about one of the most patient and docile people I know. He puts up with my mass amount of energy, my crazy ideas and my cooking experiments. He is a runner, fly fishing enthusiast, and obsessed with taking up a new hobby each week. 2. The Mayor. That's my son Aiden's nickname. For a six year old he seems to always end up in the local paper and he will talk to just about anyone. He is obsessed with Nascar and learning how everything is built. 3. My dear sweet Madelynn who in my eyes is a 3 year old prima ballerina (I am sure all parents think that about they're children). She is a lover of singing and dancing. We are STILL in the Frozen phase right now. 4. We have three cats right now. I fear we are turning into cat hoarders. They are 'large and in charge' and behave worse than the kids. But we love them. All three. 5. Gil the Gerbil. Just a relaxed friendly little guy who just hangs out. He is my favorite.

So that's a short intro into the simple life of the C family. I apologize in advance for my poor grammar and run on sentences. I will probably never share with you my weight and talk about politics and religion. In my opinion those are three very personal and explosive topics that should be kept to ourselves. My main focus will be home, health, and family. I look forward to this new journey into the blogging world!!!
